# ENCYCLOPEDIA: MAI AM GIA DINH VIET PROGRAM

## 1. General Introduction
- **Program Name**: Mai Am Gia Dinh Viet (TV Reality Version).
- **Broadcast Schedule**: Aired on HTV7 channel since October 5, 2022.
- **Production**: Ho Chi Minh City Television (HTV) in collaboration with Bee Media (Golden Moon for the first 58 episodes).
- **Partners & Sponsors**: Hoa Sen Home (Hoa Sen Group) and the Ho Chi Minh City Child Rights Protection Association.
- **Host (MC)**: Artist Quyen Linh, known for his signature stopwatch that has accompanied him for 20 years in charity programs for the poor.

## 2. Purpose and Participants
- **Origin**: Continues the 10-year charity journey of Hoa Sen Group. Created from the concern for over 4,400 children orphaned by COVID-19 and hundreds of thousands of other unfortunate children without support.
- **Target**: Initially aimed at families and children who lost parents due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Since December 2025, expanded to children in difficult circumstances (even with both parents) and freshmen.
- **Significance**: Acts as a bridge between difficult circumstances and sponsors, providing a healthy playground and educational opportunities for children.

## 3. Rules and Gameplay
Each episode features 3 teams (3 families) undergoing timed challenges.
- **Side Challenge**: 2 celebrity guests perform a side challenge. If completed on time, the 3 families receive a "priority" to reduce the difficulty of the main challenge.
- **Timed Rounds (Round 1 & 2)**: Families perform main challenges. Time is calculated from start to finish (plus penalty time for rule violations).
- **Results of Timed Rounds**:
    - **1st Place** (Lowest total time): Proceeds to the Logo Drawing Round.
    - **2nd Place**: Ends the journey with a 20 million VND prize.
    - **3rd Place**: Ends the journey with a 15 million VND prize. (Note: The first 3 episodes awarded 16m and 14m respectively).
    - **Update from Episode 126**: 3rd place family draws 1 logo board, 2nd place draws 2 logo boards (separate from the 1st place team's boards).
- **Logo Drawing Round** (For the 1st place team):
    - Family performs one final timed challenge.
    - There are 8 logo boards with different prize denominations.
    - **Under 1 min 30s**: Draw all 8 logos (maximum prize).
    - The longer the time, the fewer logos are drawn. No logos after 10 minutes.

## 4. Impressive Figures and Achievements
- **Support Results**: After 165 episodes, supported 468 families and awarded dozens of scholarships (5,000,000 VND/each) across 28 provinces. Total support reached over 38 billion VND.
- **Prestigious Awards**:
    - **Mai Vang Awards** (2022, 2023): Most Favorite Digital and TV Program.
    - **Vạn Xuân Awards** (2023, 2024, 2025): Multiple wins in CSR and Meaningful Brand categories.
    - **Kotler Award** (2024): "Excellence Impactful Community".
    - **Human Act Prize** (2025): "Inspirational Project".
